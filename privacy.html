Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Cardoodle is a privacy-first loyalty card wallet. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Storage

All loyalty card data (names, barcodes, photos, notes) is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is stored in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit — accessible only to you.

No Tracking

Cardoodle contains:

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs
  • No crash reporting services
  • No user accounts or registration

Camera and Photo Library

Cardoodle requests camera access solely to scan barcodes and photograph physical cards. Photos are stored locally on your device (or in your private iCloud account if sync is enabled). They are never transmitted to any server.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
