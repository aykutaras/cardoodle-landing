Cardoodle

An open-source, privacy-first loyalty card wallet for iOS and Apple Watch. Ditch the physical cards — scan, store, and display barcodes right from your pocket or wrist.

Zero tracking No third-party SDKs 100% open source
My Cards
Cards

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built entirely with Apple frameworks. No accounts, no ads, no telemetry.

📷

Scan or enter barcodes

Point your camera at any loyalty card barcode, or enter the number manually. Supports QR, Code128, EAN-13, EAN-8, Code39, PDF417, and Aztec.

🔍

Fullscreen display

Show barcodes at maximum screen brightness on a white background for reliable scanning at checkout — every time.

⌚

Apple Watch companion

Browse your cards and display barcodes from your wrist. Cards sync automatically from your iPhone via WatchConnectivity.

📸

Card photos and notes

Photograph the front and back of physical cards. Store account numbers, PINs, or reminders alongside each card.

☁

Optional iCloud sync

Sync cards across all your Apple devices using CloudKit's private database. Completely opt-in — works fully offline.

⭐

Pin favorites

Keep your most-used cards at the top. Search instantly by name. Customize with colors and icons.

Privacy is not a feature — it's the foundation

Cardoodle has zero third-party SDKs. Your loyalty card data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync, which uses your own private iCloud account.
